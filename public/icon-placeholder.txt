Please create the following icon files in the public directory:

1. icon-192x192.png (192x192 pixels)
2. icon-512x512.png (512x512 pixels)
3. apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 pixels)

These icons should be:
- Square in shape
- Have a safe zone in the middle 80% of the space
- Use your app's primary branding
- Be visible on both light and dark backgrounds
- For maskable icons, ensure the design extends to the edges

You can use tools like:
- Figma
- Adobe XD
- https://maskable.app/editor (for maskable icons)
- https://realfavicongenerator.net/ (for generating all required sizes)
